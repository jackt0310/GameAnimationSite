Objective

Find key in maze and escape

Controls

WASD - movement

Move mouse - turn camera

Left Click - sword attack

Move + Left Click - moving sword attack

Hold right click - draw bow

Release right click - fire bow

E - forward roll

F - Holy Hand Grenade

G - Self-destruct

R - Restart level (when dead)

Q - Change hymn (when more than one hymn unlocked)

C - Open Chests/Talk to NPCs

Esc - Pause Menu

Maze Generation

A new maze is automatically generated every time a scene is loaded (including when the player is killed and restarts the level)

Difficulty Levels

Easy

  • Infinite arrows
  • Infinite grenades
  • Ghosts move slower
  • Ghosts spawn less frequently

Medium

  • 30 arrows
  • 10 grenades
  • Ghosts move at a normal speed
  • Ghosts spawn at a normal rate

Hard

  • 15 arrows
  • 5 grenades
  • Ghosts move quickly in later levels
  • Ghosts spawn very frequently in later levels

Ghosts

Deal 50 damage to player before despawning

Drops

  • Arrows (if needed) - 8% chance
  • Grenades (if needed) - 8% chance
  • Food (if needed) - 8% chance
  • 10 gold - remaining% chance

Die in one hit

Chests

2-5 chests spawn per map

  • 2 chests - 40% chance
  • 3 chests - 30% chance
  • 4 chests - 20% chance
  • 5 chests - 10% chance

Contains:
  • 50 gold - 30% chance
  • Hymn - 30% chance
  • 100 gold - 20% chance
  • 500 gold - 7% chance
  • Arrow upgrade - 5% chance
  • Grenade upgrade - 5% chance
  • Health upgrade - 3% chance
